The Signature Mechanic

The Socratic Trap: make faking it cost more than learning.

Cheating doesn't get a failing grade based on a guess. It gets caught by the structure of the work itself.

  • ✓Keystroke dynamics. Humans type at 50–100ms per character. A paste mutates text in ~1ms — flagged instantly.
  • ✓Focus tracking. Leave to a second tab, come back, dump a wall of text — the telemetry sees it.
  • ✓Multi-turn bottleneck. Relaying answers across 5–6 adaptive rounds is slower than just learning it.
  • ✓The verification gate. A 30-second, un-googleable quiz drawn from the live session. Fake it, fail it.
